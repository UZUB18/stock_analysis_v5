# Stretchy Coffee (Chrome & FF Only)

A Pen created on CodePen.

Original URL: [https://codepen.io/jkantner/pen/Yzaxqzy](https://codepen.io/jkantner/pen/Yzaxqzy).

Who said you couldn’t stretch apart your cup for more coffee? A CSS/SVG replica of a [preloader from Dribbble](https://dribbble.com/shots/11339668-Coffee-Preloader-Animation) by Mantas Bačiuška. Since this involves animating `d`, it won’t work in Safari due to its lack of support.
